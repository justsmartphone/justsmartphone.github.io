1. Preparing to Scan#

Scanning documents on your Android phone is a simple and convenient way to manage your paperwork. Here are three tips for scanning documents quickly and efficiently.

First, make sure there is enough light in the room when scanning. To ensure accurate scans, use natural sunlight or a lamp to create a bright, even illumination. This will prevent shadows and distortion in your scans.

Second, make sure the document is flat against the surface. A slight curve on the paper will create a distorted scan. Place the document on a flat surface and press down on the edges of the document with your hands to flatten it.

Third, keep the phone directly above the document. At this angle, your phone’s camera will better capture all of the details of the document. Make sure you don’t move the camera while scanning, or your scan might be blurry.

With these tips, you can scan documents quickly and efficiently on your Android phone.
