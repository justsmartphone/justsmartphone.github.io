1. Find Model Number on the Physical Device#

When you need to look up your tablet’s model number, the first step is to find it on the physical device. If your tablet has the manufacturer’s logo printed on the back of the device, you should see a white label with the model information. This label can also be located on the side, back or bottom of your device. If your device doesn’t have the label, try using a flashlight to look inside the ports. Your model number will usually be located near the ports.

Next, you can use your tablet’s settings menu to search for the model number. Many Android tablets have a ‘Device Info’ menu which will give you the device name, hardware version, and model number. To access this menu, go to your ‘Settings’ and look for the ‘Device Info’ option.

If you are still unable to locate the model number, the next step is to use a third-party app. Apps such as Device Info HW can provide you with the model number as well as other device information. To determine which app you need, look for the app that is compatible with your device’s operating system.

Finally, if you still cannot find your model number, you can check your device’s user manual. This manual should include the model number information as well as other pertinent device information. If you cannot find the user manual, you can usually download a copy from the manufacturer’s website.

Using these steps, you can unlock the secrets to finding the model number of your Android tablet. With the model information in hand, you can easily look up helpful tutorials and operating system updates for your device.
