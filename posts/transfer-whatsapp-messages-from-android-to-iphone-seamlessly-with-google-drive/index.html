1. Preparation of Transferring WhatsApp Messages#

Are you wondering how to transfer Whatsapp messages from an Android device to an iPhone using Google Drive? You may think that it’s impossible, but with a few simple steps, you can easily transfer your Whatsapp messages from Android to iPhone using Google Drive. Firstly, make sure that you have Google Drive installed on both your Android and iOS devices. Once you have Google Drive installed on both devices, you can begin the process of transferring your Whatsapp messages.

Next, open the Whatsapp app on both your Android and iOS devices and tap on the “Settings” option. Scroll down and tap on the “Chats” option and then tap on the “Chat Backup” option. Now, tap on the “Back Up” button and select Google Drive as the preferred backup option. Then, choose the frequency of your backups and enter the Google account details.

Once your Whatsapp messages have been backed up to your Google Drive account, you can easily restore them to your iOS device. To do this, simply open the Whatsapp app on your iOS device and tap on the “Settings” option. Then, scroll down and tap on the “Chats” option and then tap on the “Chat Backup” option. Now, tap on the “Restore” button and enter your Google account details.

That’s it! You have now successfully transferred your Whatsapp messages from Android to iPhone using Google Drive. All your Whatsapp messages, including media, will be transferred to your iOS device.
