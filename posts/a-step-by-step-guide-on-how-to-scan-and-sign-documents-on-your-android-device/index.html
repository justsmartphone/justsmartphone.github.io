Second, tap on the ‘Scan’ button in the Camera app to turn the document into an image. The app will process the image and will allow you to adjust the boundaries and delete the unwanted portions of the scanned image.

Third, after you are done with scanning the document, tap on the ‘Done’ button in the Camera app. This will store the scanned document in your device memory in the chosen file format. You can also rename the document before saving it.

Finally, open any of the document editor app such as Google Drive, Dropbox etc. to make additional edits to the document like adding signature or highlighting some text. After you finish all the necessary editing, you can share the document with anyone or store it in the cloud storage.
