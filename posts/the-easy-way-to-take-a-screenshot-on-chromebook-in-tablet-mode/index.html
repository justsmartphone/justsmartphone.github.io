If you want to take a screenshot while you’re in tablet mode on your Chromebook, it’s easy to do. Here’s how to enter Tablet Mode and easily take a screenshot.

To enter into Tablet Mode and start taking screenshots, simply rotate your Chromebook’s display hinge from the standard laptop position to a flat tablet position. Your Chromebook will detect this change and switch into Tablet Mode automatically. Now that you’re in tablet mode, you can easily take a screenshot by pressing the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Window Switcher’ keys located at the top of the keyboard at the same time.

To view the screenshot, just access your Chromebook’s ‘Files’ app, and select the ‘Screenshots’ folder from the left-hand column. Here, you will be able to see all screenshots that you have taken in tablet mode. You can also use this folder to move screenshots to other apps or share them with friends and family.

Finally, to exit Tablet Mode, simply rotate your Chromebook’s display back to the upright laptop position, and it will automatically switch back to regular laptop mode. And that’s all there is to it- with these easy steps you’ll be taking screenshots in tablet mode on your Chromebook in no time.

1. Activating Tablet Mode#

Entering into Tablet Mode on a Chromebook gives users an alternative way of interacting with their device. To enable Tablet Mode, you can either press the power button which will toggle Tablet Mode on and off, or follow the steps below.

Firstly, press the three dots located in the bottom right corner to bring up the Settings menu. From here, click on Device and then look for the Allow Tablet Mode option and toggle it on. Once this is enabled, pressing the power button once will automatically activate Tablet Mode.

Enabling Tablet Mode will change the user interface to allow for a more touch-friendly experience. Instead of having a close-minimize-maximize button in the top right corner, the ‘X’ button at the bottom of the window will close the application. Additionally, the start menu will be removed and a home button will be shown at the bottom of the screen instead.

Lastly, it is important to note that when you enter Tablet Mode on your Chromebook, the option to take a screenshot in tablet mode is still available. To take a screenshot while in Tablet Mode, press the power button and the volume down button at the same time and it will save the entire screen to your Chromebook’s Downloads folder.
