Using Google Drive, it is easy and hassle-free to transfer your WhatsApp chat conversation from Android to iPhone. It just requires a few steps and you’re done. Now, you need to move to the iPhone. Open WhatsApp and during the registration process, enter your same phone number as used on the Android device. When you get to the restore screen, tap on the “Restore” button. This will begin the restoration process which will pull the chat conversation from your Google Drive.

When the restoration is done, you should be able to see all the chat conversations from the Android phone on your iPhone.
