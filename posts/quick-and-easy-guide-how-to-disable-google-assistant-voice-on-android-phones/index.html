Here’s a quick and easy guide on how to disable Google Assistant Voice on Android phones.

The first step is to open up the Google App on your Android phone and click on the menu icon, which is usually represented by three horizontal lines. This will open up a list of options, and you should select Settings. Once there, scroll down to the Accounts & Privacy page and click on it.

Then scroll down to the Google Assistant section and select Phone from the list. This will open up an options page for you where you can turn off Google Assistant Voice. Simply toggle the switch at the top of the page and your Google Assistant Voice will be disabled.

Finally, you can also look for the Voice Match section, which is located at the bottom of the page. Here, you can uncheck the box to turn off the voice activation feature on your device. This will ensure that Google Assistant Voice will not activate when you are speaking nearby.

By following these steps, you can easily disable Google Assistant Voice from your Android phone. This way, you can protect your privacy from intrusive voice activation, and reduce the amount of battery that Google Assistant Voice uses by not having it running in the background.

Here’s a quick and easy guide that explains how to disable Google Assistant Voice on Android phones.

First, open the Google app. Then, select the three-dot menu icon in the top-right corner and select Settings. In the Google Assistant menu, choose Phone. Then, switch the toggle under Google Assistant voice off. You’ve now successfully turned off the Google Assistant voice function.

Alternatively, you can turn off Google Assistant Voice directly from the home screen. Simply long-press the Home button to launch the assistant. Then, select the Explore icon in the bottom-right corner. Finally, select the three-dot menu icon and select Settings. After that, tap the active toggle next to Google Assistant and turn it off.

If you want to keep Google Assistant Voice active for certain tasks, you can customize it to respond only to a special voice command.
