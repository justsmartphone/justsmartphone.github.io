A3: Some tips to extend your Airpod battery life include turning off “Hey Siri” to save power, keeping the Airpods at room temperature, and disabling automatic ear detection. Q4: What steps should I follow if my Airpods won’t hold a charge? A4: If your Airpods won’t hold a charge, try cleaning the contacts on the Airpods and charging case with a soft, dry, lint-free cloth. Additionally, you can reset your Airpods by pressing and holding the setup button on the back of the charging case for 15 seconds.

Q5: What should I do if my Airpod battery is draining quickly? A5: If your Airpod battery is draining quickly, try disabling ‘Hey Siri’ and keeping your Airpods at room temperature.
