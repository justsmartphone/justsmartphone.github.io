Q: How do I take a screenshot on my Insignia Android tablet? A: To take a screenshot on an Insignia Android tablet, press the Volume Down and Power/Lock buttons simultaneously for two seconds and release. Your device will capture the screenshot.

Q: Where are my screenshots saved? A: By default, screenshots taken on an Insignia Android tablet are saved in the Pictures/Screenshots folder. To view and edit your screenshots, open them in the Gallery app.
