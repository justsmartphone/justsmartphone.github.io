Screen mirroring is a feature that allows users to share content from their phone or laptop to their TV. To take advantage of this feature, users need an Android TV that supports screen mirroring. This guide will explain how to use the feature to unlock the magic of screen mirroring with an Android TV.

The first step is to ensure that both the device and the TV are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Next, launch the Settings app on the Android TV and select Connected Devices. Enable Screen Mirroring by selecting the toggle and then select the device you want to connect. On the device you are mirroring, open the Notification Center and select the Screen Mirroring tab. Select the Android TV you want to connect and follow the on-screen instructions to establish the connection.

Once the connection is established, whatever you do on the phone or laptop will be mirrored to the Android TV.
