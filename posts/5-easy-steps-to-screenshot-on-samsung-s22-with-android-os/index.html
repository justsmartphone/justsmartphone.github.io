Q3: Is there any other way to take a screenshot on my Samsung S22? A3: Yes, you can also take a screenshot on your Samsung S22 using the Smart Select feature. To do this, you just need to swipe the edge of your hand across the screen and select the ‘Screenshot’ option.

Q4: Where do I go to adjust the screenshot settings on my Samsung S22? A4: To adjust the screenshot settings on your Samsung S22, open the Settings app and tap on “Display”.
