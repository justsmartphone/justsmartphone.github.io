Q: How do I take a screenshot on my HP Pavilion laptop?

A: To take a screenshot on your HP Pavilion laptop, press the “Windows + PrtScn” keys simultaneously. This will save the screenshot to the Windows 10 Captures folder.

Q: Where can I find the screenshot I just took?

A: Once you have taken the screenshot, it will be saved to the Captures folder in Windows 10. This can be accessed by pressing the Windows key, typing “Captures” and clicking on the folder.

Q: What file type do screenshots on my HP Pavilion laptop use?

A: Screenshots on your HP Pavilion laptop are saved as PNG files by default.

Q: Can I change the file type of the screenshots I take?

A: Yes, you can change the file type of the screenshots you take on your HP Pavilion laptop. To do this, open the Captures folder and open the screenshot you want to change. Then go to File > Save As > and select your desired file type.
