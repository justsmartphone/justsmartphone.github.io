First, go to ‘Settings’ on your tablet. You will see several categories such as ‘Device’, ‘Personal’, and ‘Connections’. Select ‘Application Manager’ in the ‘Device’ section.

You will be taken to a page showing all the apps installed on your device. Select ‘All’ at the top of the page to view system apps. Scroll through the list to locate the ‘Download Manager’. Tap it and then select ‘Clear Cache’.

The cache for the Download Manager will now be cleared. To clear more caches, go back to the Application Manager menu and select ‘Clear Cache’ for any other app you wish to delete the cache for.

Finally, reboot your device to apply the changes. Clearing the cache on a Samsung Android tablet is a simple task that requires only a few steps. Keeping your device’s cache clean can help improve performance and keep your device running smoothly.
