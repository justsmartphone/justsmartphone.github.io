First of all, you need to open the window you intend to screenshot. Once the window is open, press the ‘Print Screen’ key on your keyboard. This will capture the entire window. Next, open the ‘Downloads’ folder on your laptop. You should see the screenshot that you just took. At this point, you can go ahead and edit the screenshot if you want. To do this, you can use the built-in tools in Google Chrome or install an external image editor.

When you’re happy with the image, you can save it to a folder on your laptop or share it with others. To save the image, simply right-click on the image and select ‘Save As’. This will open a window where you can choose the destination folder and the file format. After that, click ‘Save’ and the image will be saved to your laptop.
