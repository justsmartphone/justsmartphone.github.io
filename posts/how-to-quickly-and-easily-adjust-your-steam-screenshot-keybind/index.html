If you’re familiar with Steam, you know that taking screenshots is one of its most useful features. By default, your Steam screenshot keybind is the F12 key, but it can be changed easily. Here are steps to change steam screenshot keybind:

First, open Steam and go to the Settings menu. Click on the In-Game tab and then the Screenshots section. Uncheck the Enable the Steam Overlay while in-game box. This will also disable the default F12 keybind.

Second, open the game for which you want to change the keybind. In the game’s main menu, go to the Options or Settings menu to find the Keybinds page. Here, you can assign a new keybind to the steam screenshot action.

Third, press the key or series of keys that you want to assign as the Steam Screenshot keybind. It will show up in the Keybinds page as “Steam Screenshot” or something similar.

Finally, press the Apply or Done button to save your new Steam Screenshot keybind. Now, you can take Steam Screenshot by pressing the key you just assigned.
