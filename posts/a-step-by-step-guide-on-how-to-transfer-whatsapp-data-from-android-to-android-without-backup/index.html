Transferring data from one Android device to another can be a difficult task, especially if you don’t have the right tools or don’t have a backup. This can become especially frustrating when it comes to Whatsapp. However, there is a way to transfer Whatsapp data from one Android device to another without backup.

First, both devices must be connected to the same Wi-Fi connection. Then, the user on the sending device must open their Whatsapp and go to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup. Here, the user will need to make a backup of their Whatsapp data and select “Google Drive” as the storage location.

Next, on the receiving device, the user must open their Whatsapp and open Settings > Chats > Chat Backup. From there, they must select “Restore” and choose the backup to restore the data. Once completed, the Whatsapp data will be transferred from one device to another.

By following these steps, users will be able to easily transfer Whatsapp data from one Android device to another without the need for a backup. With this easy method, users can continue using their Whatsapp conversations on their new device with ease.
