2. Capturing and Processing Documents with the Camera#

Scanning documents with your Samsung Galaxy S21 should be a seamless and secure experience. Here are a few tips to get the best experience possible when using your device to scan documents.

First, you’ll want to make sure that your settings are optimized for document scanning. In the Camera app, tap the Settings icon (the gear) and check the options for Decor and Object Removal. These settings can help make sure you don’t get any blurry or distorted images.

Next, use the Flash setting to ensure that you can capture the document in all its detail. The flash will provide a more even lighting, reducing glare and making the document appear more clearly.

Finally, make sure you are holding your phone steady when you are scanning. A slight movement can cause the document to appear blurry or distorted. If you have trouble keeping your phone steady, you can use a tripod or other stabilizing device for better results.

By following these tips, you should be able to get the best results possible when scanning documents with your Samsung Galaxy S21!
