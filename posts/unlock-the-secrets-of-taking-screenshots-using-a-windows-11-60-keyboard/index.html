To take a screenshot of your entire screen, press the ‘Windows Key’ + ‘PrtScn’ (Print Screen). This will capture a screenshot of your entire screen and save it to your Pictures > Screenshots folder. You can quickly access this folder by pressing the Windows Key + E keyboard shortcut.

To take a screenshot of a single window, press the ‘Alt’ + ‘PrtScn’ keys. This will take a picture of your active window and store it in the Screenshots folder. You can now open the image file and further edit it, if required, using a photo-editing tool like Microsoft Paint.

Finally, if you need to take a screenshot of a specific area of your screen, press the ‘Windows Key’ + ‘Shift’ + ‘S’. This will open the Snipping Tool, which allows you to draw a box around the area you would like to capture. Once you select a section of the screen, it will be automatically stored in your Screenshots folder.
