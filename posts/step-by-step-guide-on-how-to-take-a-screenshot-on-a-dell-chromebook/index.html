If you’re using a Dell Chromebook, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you take a screenshot.

First, press the “Ctrl” and “window switch” key simultaneously. This two-key combination will take a shot of the whole screen. To take a screenshot of just a part of the screen, press “Ctrl,” “Shift,” and “Window switch” together. Once you’ve done this, you’ll be able to select a region of the screen to capture.

Next, open a Chrome browser window and type “Ctrl + F5.” This will open a new window which you can use to view your screenshot. If you want to save your screenshot, press “Ctrl + S” and specify where you’d like to save the file.

Finally, you can use the “Ctrl + P” shortcut to print your screenshot. This will open a new window containing your image, which you can then print.
