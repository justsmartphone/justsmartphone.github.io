First, make sure your iPhone and Android device are both turned on and their Bluetooth is enabled. Then, pair your devices by going into your iPhone’s settings and selecting “Bluetooth”. If your Android device is discoverable, your iPhone will detect it and you can pair them.

After the contacts have been transferred, you can verify they were received correctly by opening your Android contacts app and seeing if the contacts are listed. You can also delete the transferred contacts from your iPhone if you don’t want to keep them on both devices.
