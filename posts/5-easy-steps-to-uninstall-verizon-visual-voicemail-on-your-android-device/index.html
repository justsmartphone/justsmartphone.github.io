Q: How do I uninstall Verizon Visual Voicemail from my Android device? A: To uninstall Verizon Visual Voicemail from your Android device, you will need to open your device’s Settings app. From there, tap on Apps & Notifications, then select the Visual Voicemail app. From the app information page, you can tap on the Uninstall button to remove the app from your device.

Q: Are there any potential risks to uninstalling Verizon Visual Voicemail? A: Uninstalling Verizon Visual Voicemail may cause you to lose access to any voicemail messages stored within the app. Additionally, any outstanding call forwarding or voicemail-to-text settings will be reset.
